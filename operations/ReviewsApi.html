getHotelReviews

The retrieval of hotel reviews can be customized by providing various query parameters. These parameters include sorting options, page number, page size, and a search term. Sample request: GET /hotels/{hotelId}/reviews?sortOrder=desc&sortColumn=creationDate&pageNumber=1&pageSize=5&searchQuery=Excellent
